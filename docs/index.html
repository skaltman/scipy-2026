Correctness matters in data analysis

and so do reproducibility and transparency

…all often at odds with how LLMs behave

we already mentioned how “correctness matters”. reproducibility – get consistent results from same conditions – and transparency – insight into how an analysis was done – are also central to data science, all often at odds with the nondetermistic and black box nature of LLMs.
